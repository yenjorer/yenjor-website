ABOUT YENJOR

We Are The Leading Button Manufacturer in The World.

Yenjor Co., Ltd. was established in 2003 and currently covers an area of 3000 square meters with over 100 employees. Located in the center of the Yangtze River Delta urban agglomeration of Shanghai, Hangzhou, Suzhou, and Ningbo, with convenient transportation and superior location.

The main production and development of clothing accessories include metal button, resin button, natural material button, snap button, jeans button, rivets, pants hooks, adjustable button, prong snap button, Eyelet, and so on. It has automated die-casting workshops, rolling plating workshops, hanging plating workshops, painting workshops, packaging finished product workshops, and so on.
