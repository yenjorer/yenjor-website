Crafting Excellence

Founded in 2005, Yenjor has evolved from a small workshop into one of China's leading manufacturers of premium buttons and garment accessories. Our journey is defined by an unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

With nearly two decades of expertise, we serve renowned brands and garment factories across 80+ countries. Our philosophy is simple: every detail matters, every button tells a story of craftsmanship.
