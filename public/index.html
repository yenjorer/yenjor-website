ABOUT US

Two Decades of Excellence in Button Manufacturing

Located in the renowned button production base - Jiashan County, Zhejiang Province, Yenjor Co., Ltd. has been at the forefront of button manufacturing since 2003.

We specialize in producing a wide range of high-quality buttons including shell buttons, metal buttons, resin buttons, coconut buttons, and more. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for fashion brands and garment factories worldwide.
